--- HOW TO SETUP: ---
1. Download the latest MelonLoader Installer from here:
https://github.com/LavaGang/MelonLoader.Installer/releases/latest/download/MelonLoader.Installer.exe

2. Open the installer and find the game "Baby Steps"
3. Click the "Enable Nightly Builds" check box, then click the Install button
4. Drag the BabyStepsMultiplayerClient.dll and LiteNetLib.dll files into the "Mods" folder in the game's main directory (can be found through Steam or via the folder button in the MelonLoader installer program)
5. Launch the game normally and wait for first startup init to take place

--- How to use the mod: ---
Once you have loaded into a save and are in game, pause the game and press "F2" to open the Multiplayer Mod Menu.

You must type in a valid Server IP and Port to connect with other players.

You can type in a username and adjust the sliders to pick a custom color for your player. White is the default gray color of the onesie.

Click "Connect" to join a server, and Disconnect to leave a server. Click "Update Appearance" to change your color and username while connected.

Click "Enable/Disable Collisions" to not interact with other players physically.
